Hypnosis for Weight-Loss

I am often asked "Does Hypnotherapy work for weight loss"? The simple answer is yes. Weight loss can be a difficult thing to sustain. Our eating habits are often long standing and linked closely with our emotions. Hypnotherapy for weight loss can help by breaking the link between emotions and eating for comfort, safety and reward. Using weight loss hypnosis I will give you a new perspective when thinking about food. Instead of it being used for comfort or to fulfill an emotional need you will start to see food for its nutritious and life supporting nature. I will give you skills and techniques to support your new way of eating to help you change your current unhealthy habits and practises. You will want to choose healthy nutritious food to feed yourself with. The resulting weight loss due to these changes will lead to feeling more in control and empowered and this will become your new reward.
